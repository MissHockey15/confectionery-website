About

We at Beaus Bonbons are the oldest family-owned business in the Ottawa Valley and a prime destination in the heart of Ottawa. Under the supervision of Master Chocolatier and Pastry Chef Heinrich Stubbe, We strive to create unimaginable products hand in hand with our young chefs from Le Cordon Bleu Ottawa Culinary Arts Institute.

Products

  • Truffles

    Experience flavour with our signature truffles, hand crafted the same way we did six generations ago. Choose from over 39 exquisite flavours of dark, milk and white. 52%, 70% and 100% cocoa coupled with hand picked flavours and spices. We offer boxes of: 2, 4, 6, 9, 12, 15, 24, 48.

  • Bars

    The bars at Beau’s Bonbons come in a range of white, milk, and two types of dark chocolate (54% semi sweet and a 70% bitter sweet).These are available with 7 different toppings: Dried Cranberries, Blueberries or Cherries, Almonds, Pistachios, Hazelnuts or Roasted Cocoa Nibs.

  • Bonbons

    A delightful combination of fruits, spices, caramel, almonds or hazelnut paste with Chef Heinrich's experiences in the world of chocolate.

  • Cake

    Our passion for baking at Stubbe Chocolates is reflected in our Tortes and Cakes, with their light and creamy ganache, rich chocolate mousse, buttercream, sponge cake and other delicious additions. Hand-decorated by our in-house cake artists, every occasion can be extra special  with our customized cakes and tortes.
